RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT (MONTH-TO-MONTH)

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made between Cedar Point Residential LLC ("Landlord") and Riley Example ("Tenant") for the apartment located at 88 Harborview Court, Unit 2B, Lakeside (the "Premises"). This is a fictional document created for demonstration purposes.

1. TERM. This lease continues on a month-to-month basis, beginning on March 1, and renews automatically each month until terminated as described in Section 5.

2. RENT. Tenant agrees to pay rent of $1,275.00 per month, plus a month-to-month premium of $50.00 per month, for a total monthly payment of $1,325.00, due on the first day of each month, payable by electronic transfer to the account designated by Landlord in writing.

3. LATE FEES. Rent received more than five (5) days after the due date is subject to a late fee of $60.00. A returned payment will incur a $30.00 returned-payment fee.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT. Tenant has paid a security deposit of $1,275.00, to be returned within fourteen (14) days after move-out, less lawful deductions for damage beyond normal wear and tear or unpaid amounts owed under this Agreement.

5. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate this lease for any reason by giving the other party at least thirty (30) days' written notice, effective at the end of a monthly rental period. No lease-break penalty applies.

6. UTILITIES. Tenant is responsible for electricity and internet. Landlord shall pay for water, sewer, trash collection, and building common-area electricity.

7. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS. Landlord is responsible for maintaining the building structure, plumbing, heating, and appliances supplied with the unit. Tenant shall keep the Premises clean, use fixtures and appliances reasonably, and promptly report any needed repairs.

8. ENTRY BY LANDLORD. Landlord may enter the Premises to make repairs, conduct inspections, or show the unit, after providing at least twenty-four (24) hours' written notice, except in an emergency.

9. OCCUPANCY. The Premises shall be occupied only by Tenant and household members listed on the application. Subletting or short-term rental of the Premises is not permitted without Landlord's prior written consent.

10. PARKING. One (1) assigned parking space is included with the Premises at no additional charge. Guest parking is available in marked visitor spaces only.

11. SMOKING. Smoking is not permitted anywhere inside the Premises or in any common area of the building.

12. QUIET ENJOYMENT. Tenant is entitled to the quiet enjoyment of the Premises and shall not disturb other residents or use the Premises for any unlawful purpose.

13. RENTER'S INSURANCE. Tenant shall maintain a renter's insurance policy with personal liability coverage of at least $100,000.00 for the duration of the tenancy and shall provide proof of coverage to Landlord upon request.

14. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Agreement contains the entire understanding of the parties. Any changes must be made in writing and signed by both Landlord and Tenant.

Signed: Cedar Point Residential LLC (Landlord) and Riley Example (Tenant).
