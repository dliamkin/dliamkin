RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT (RENEWAL)

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made between Maple & Vine Property Group ("Landlord") and Jordan Sample ("Tenant") for the property located at 4127 Willow Bend Lane, Springfield (the "Premises"). This is a fictional document created for demonstration purposes.

1. TERM. The term of this lease is twelve (12) months, beginning on August 1 and ending on July 31 of the following year.

2. RENT. Tenant agrees to pay rent of $1,595.00 per month, due on the first day of each month. Rent shall be paid by check or electronic transfer to Landlord at the address or account designated in writing.

3. LATE FEES. If rent is not received within five (5) days after the due date, Tenant shall pay a late fee of $50.00. A returned payment will incur a $35.00 returned-payment fee.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT. Tenant has paid a security deposit of $1,450.00. The deposit will be returned within thirty (30) days after move-out, less any lawful deductions for damage beyond normal wear and tear, unpaid rent, or cleaning required to restore the Premises to its move-in condition.

5. UTILITIES. Landlord shall pay for sewer and trash collection service. Tenant is responsible for water, electricity, natural gas, internet, and any other services Tenant chooses to establish at the Premises.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS. Landlord is responsible for keeping the structure, roof, plumbing, heating, and electrical systems in good working order. Tenant shall be responsible for lawn care, including mowing and seasonal trimming of the yard. Tenant shall keep the Premises clean and sanitary, promptly report needed repairs, and pay for any damage caused by Tenant's negligence or misuse.

7. OCCUPANCY AND GUESTS. The Premises shall be occupied only by Tenant and the household members listed on the application. Guests may stay no longer than seven (7) consecutive days without Landlord's prior written consent.

8. ENTRY BY LANDLORD. Landlord may enter the Premises to make repairs, conduct inspections, or show the property, after providing at least twenty-four (24) hours' written notice, except in an emergency.

9. NOTICE TO VACATE. Tenant shall provide at least sixty (60) days' written notice before the end of the lease term if Tenant does not intend to remain in the Premises.

10. SMOKING. Smoking is not permitted anywhere inside the Premises or within ten (10) feet of any entrance.

11. ALTERATIONS. Tenant shall not paint, wallpaper, or make structural changes to the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent. Approved alterations become the property of Landlord unless otherwise agreed in writing.

12. QUIET ENJOYMENT. Tenant is entitled to the quiet enjoyment of the Premises. Tenant shall not disturb neighbors or use the Premises for any unlawful purpose.

13. RENTER RESPONSIBILITY FOR DAMAGES. Tenant is responsible for the cost of repairing any damage caused by Tenant, household members, or guests, beyond normal wear and tear.

14. PET FEE. Tenant may keep up to one (1) approved pet at the Premises. Tenant shall pay a pet fee of $35.00 per month in addition to rent for the duration of the pet's residence.

15. EARLY TERMINATION. If Tenant terminates this lease before the end of the term, Tenant shall pay an early-termination fee equal to two (2) months' rent, in addition to rent owed through the termination date.

16. AUTOMATIC RENEWAL. Unless either party gives written notice as described in Section 9, this lease automatically renews for an additional twelve (12) month term at the then-current rent plus any increase communicated by Landlord at least sixty (60) days before renewal.

17. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Agreement contains the entire understanding of the parties. Any changes must be made in writing and signed by both Landlord and Tenant.

Signed: Maple & Vine Property Group (Landlord) and Jordan Sample (Tenant).
