RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made between Maple & Vine Property Group ("Landlord") and Jordan Sample ("Tenant") for the property located at 4127 Willow Bend Lane, Springfield (the "Premises"). This is a fictional document created for demonstration purposes.

1. TERM. The term of this lease is twelve (12) months, beginning on September 1, 2026 and ending on August 31, 2027. At the end of the term, this lease ends unless both parties sign a new agreement.

2. RENT. Tenant agrees to pay rent of $1,595.00 per month, due on the first day of each month. Rent shall be paid by check or electronic transfer to Landlord at the address or account designated in writing.

3. LATE FEES. If rent is not received within five (5) days after the due date, Tenant shall pay a late fee of $50.00. A returned payment will incur a $35.00 returned-payment fee.

4. NON-RENEWAL NOTICE. If Tenant does not intend to renew this lease, Tenant must deliver written notice of non-renewal to Landlord at least sixty (60) days before the end of the lease term. If such notice is not received by that date, Tenant shall be responsible for a holdover administration fee of $200.00.

5. RENEWAL OFFER. Landlord will deliver a written renewal offer to Tenant within thirty (30) days after the Anniversary Date, as that term is defined in the Master Property Schedule maintained by Landlord. Tenant is encouraged to review the offer promptly upon receipt.

6. MOVE-OUT INSPECTION. A move-out inspection is scheduled for September 1, 2027 at the Premises. Tenant may be present and should ensure the Premises are returned to their move-in condition, less normal wear and tear.

7. SECURITY DEPOSIT. Tenant has paid a security deposit of $1,595.00. The deposit will be returned within thirty (30) days after the move-out inspection, less any lawful deductions for damage beyond normal wear and tear, unpaid rent, or required cleaning.

8. UTILITIES. Tenant is responsible for electricity, natural gas, water, internet, and any other services Tenant chooses to establish at the Premises. Landlord shall pay for trash collection service.

9. ENTRY BY LANDLORD. Landlord may enter the Premises to make repairs, conduct inspections, or show the property, after providing at least twenty-four (24) hours' written notice, except in an emergency.

10. QUIET ENJOYMENT. Tenant is entitled to the quiet enjoyment of the Premises. Tenant shall not disturb neighbors or use the Premises for any unlawful purpose.

11. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Agreement contains the entire understanding of the parties. Any changes must be made in writing and signed by both Landlord and Tenant.

Signed: Maple & Vine Property Group (Landlord) and Jordan Sample (Tenant).
