Auto Policy Summary — Private Passenger Vehicle

This summary describes the key terms of your auto insurance policy. It is not a complete statement of coverage; refer to the full policy for details. This is a fictional document created for demonstration purposes.

POLICY PERIOD. Your policy is effective from July 15, 2026 through January 14, 2027. Coverage ends at 12:01 a.m. on the expiration date unless the policy is renewed.

PREMIUM. Your total premium for the policy period is $744.00, payable in monthly installments of $124.00 due on the fifteenth (15th) day of each month.

GRACE PERIOD. If an installment is not received by its due date, coverage continues during a ten (10) day grace period. This grace period is informational; a payment received after the grace period may result in cancellation for non-payment.

CANCELLATION BY POLICYHOLDER. You may cancel this policy at any time. To avoid a short-rate cancellation penalty, written notice of cancellation must be received at least thirty (30) days before the policy expiration date.

RENEWAL. A renewal offer, including any premium changes, will be mailed to your address on file on or before December 1, 2026. If you take no action, the policy renews automatically on the terms stated in the renewal offer.

CLAIMS. Claims must be reported promptly. Late reporting may affect coverage. Contact the claims department through your policyholder portal or the number on your insurance card.
