Congratulations! You have been selected to receive the Harborview Merit Scholarship in the amount of $6,000 for the upcoming academic year. This is a fictional document created for demonstration purposes.

2. ENROLLMENT VERIFICATION. The fall semester begins on September 8, 2026. You must submit proof of full-time enrollment to the Foundation within fourteen (14) days after the start of the fall semester. Verification may be a registrar's letter or an official enrollment certificate.

5. PRIOR DEADLINES. Please note that the priority housing grant application associated with this program closed on June 1, 2026, and late applications are not accepted.

6. CHANGES IN ENROLLMENT. You must notify the Foundation in writing within thirty (30) days of any change in your enrollment status, including a reduction below full-time status, a leave of absence, or a transfer to another institution.

We are delighted to support your studies. If you have questions about any requirement in this letter, contact the Office of Scholarships and Awards.
