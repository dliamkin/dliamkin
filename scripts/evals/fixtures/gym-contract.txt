IRONWORKS FITNESS — MEMBERSHIP AGREEMENT

This Membership Agreement is between Ironworks Fitness ("Club") and the undersigned member. This is a fictional document created for demonstration purposes.

1. INITIAL TERM. Your membership begins on March 2, 2026 and continues for an initial term ending on March 1, 2027. After the initial term, the membership continues month-to-month until cancelled.

2. DUES. Monthly dues of $54.00 are drafted on the fifth (5th) day of each month from the payment method on file.

3. CANCELLATION. To cancel effective at the end of the initial term, the Club must receive your written cancellation notice at least forty-five (45) days before the end of the initial term. Notices received after that date take effect forty-five (45) days after receipt, and dues continue to draft until then.

4. ANNUAL MAINTENANCE FEE. An annual facility maintenance fee of $49.00 is drafted each year on October 1.

5. FREEZE POLICY. Memberships may be frozen for up to three (3) months per calendar year with written notice. Dues are reduced to $10.00 per month while frozen.
