RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Agreement") is made between Harborlight Homes LLC ("Landlord") and Casey Placeholder ("Tenant") for the apartment located at 12 Foxglove Terrace, Unit 4, Brookmere (the "Premises"). This is a fictional document created for demonstration purposes.

1. TERM. The term of this lease is twelve (12) months, beginning on September 1 and ending on August 31 of the following year.

2. RENT. Tenant agrees to pay rent of $1,350.00 per month, due on the first day of each month, payable by check or electronic transfer to the account designated by Landlord in writing.

3. LATE FEES. A late fee of $75.00 shall apply to any rent payment not received within five (5) days after its due date. Any returned payment shall be subject to a returned-payment fee of $30.00.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT. Tenant has paid a security deposit of $1,350.00. The deposit will be returned within thirty (30) days after move-out, less any lawful deductions for damage beyond normal wear and tear or unpaid amounts owed under this Agreement.

5. UTILITIES. Tenant is responsible for electricity and internet. Landlord shall pay for water, sewer, and trash collection service.

6. MAINTENANCE AND REPAIRS. Landlord shall keep the building structure, roof, plumbing, heating, and electrical systems in good working order. Tenant agrees to keep the Premises in a clean and sanitary condition, to use fixtures and appliances in a reasonable manner, and to notify Landlord promptly of any repairs that are needed.

7. ENTRY BY LANDLORD. Landlord may enter the Premises to make repairs, conduct inspections, or show the unit, after providing at least twenty-four (24) hours' written notice, except in an emergency.

8. OCCUPANCY. The Premises shall be occupied only by Tenant and the household members listed on the application. Subletting or short-term rental of the Premises is not permitted without Landlord's prior written consent.

9. PETS. No pets are permitted at the Premises without Landlord's prior written consent.

10. SMOKING. Smoking is not permitted anywhere inside the Premises or in any common area of the building.

11. QUIET ENJOYMENT. Tenant is entitled to the quiet enjoyment of the Premises and shall not disturb other residents or use the Premises for any unlawful purpose.

12. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Agreement contains the entire understanding of the parties. Any changes must be made in writing and signed by both Landlord and Tenant.

Signed: Harborlight Homes LLC (Landlord) and Casey Placeholder (Tenant).
