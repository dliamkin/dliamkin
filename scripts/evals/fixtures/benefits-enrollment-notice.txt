EMPLOYEE BENEFITS ENROLLMENT NOTICE

Welcome to the team! This notice summarizes the enrollment requirements for your employee benefits. This is a fictional document created for demonstration purposes.

HEALTH PLAN ENROLLMENT. New employees must complete health plan enrollment within sixty (60) days of their hire date. If enrollment is not completed within this window, the next opportunity to enroll is the annual open enrollment period.

RETIREMENT PLAN. You will be automatically enrolled in the retirement savings plan at a 4% contribution rate ninety (90) days after your hire date unless you opt out or choose a different rate before then.

BENEFICIARY DESIGNATION. Please designate your beneficiaries in the benefits portal at your earliest convenience. There is no deadline for this step, but coverage determinations use the designation on file.

Questions? Contact the People Operations service desk through the employee portal.
