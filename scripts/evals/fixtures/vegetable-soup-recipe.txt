HEARTY VEGETABLE SOUP

Serves 6. Total time: about 55 minutes.

Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 yellow onion, diced
- 3 carrots, sliced
- 2 celery stalks, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
- 6 cups vegetable broth
- 1 cup green beans, trimmed
- 2 zucchini, chopped
- 1 can white beans, drained
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery and cook until softened, about 8 minutes.
2. Stir in the garlic and thyme and cook for 1 minute.
3. Add the crushed tomatoes and broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Add the green beans, zucchini, and white beans. Simmer another 15 minutes until tender.
5. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot with crusty bread.

Leftovers keep in the refrigerator for up to four days, or freeze for up to three months.
